Format used by this editor

0x0000-0x0003: little-endian CRC-32 of bytes 0x0004-0x1fff.

0x0004-0x07d3: 2,000 one-byte stats, indices 0-1999.

0x07d4-0x1f3b: 1,498 little-endian 32-bit stats, indices 2000-3497.

0x1f3c-0x1fff: 196-byte trailing area, preserved unchanged.
